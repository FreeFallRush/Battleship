Battleship

How to Play

🖥️ Desktop only: Drag and drop your ships on the player board and press "R" to rotate.
📱 Mobile: Ships are placed randomly for both players.
🚢 Each player has 7 ships: 1 Carrier (5), 1 Battleship (4), 1 Cruiser (3), 2 Destroyers (2), 2 Submarines (1).
🎯 Click on the computer's board to attack.
🔥 Hit = red, ❌ Miss = white.
🏆 First to sink all enemy ships wins!
